Executive Summary & Bottom Line

ANALYSIS BOTTOM LINE: We assess with HIGH CONFIDENCE that the threat actor operating under the persona Handala (also known as the Handala Hack Team) is a state-aligned front group operated by **Iran's Ministry of Intelligence and Security (MOIS)**, specifically corresponding to the activity cluster tracked as **Void Manticore** (Storm-0842 / Banished Kitten).

While Handala presents itself as an independent, pro-Palestinian hacktivist collective, their operational synchronization with Iranian geopolitical objectives, use of bespoke destructive wipers (e.g., BiBi, Cl, Hamsa), high-level coordination with other MOIS groups (e.g., Scarred Manticore), and recent shift to sophisticated, non-monetized "Living-off-the-Cloud" (LotL) remote-wipe capabilities (such as the Intune compromise at Stryker in March 2026) are highly inconsistent with grassroots hacktivism. The group’s primary objective is not financial gain or simple defacement, but rather psychological warfare, data destruction, and reputational damage to Israeli and Western interests.

This report documents Handala's operational workflow, maps TTPs to the MITRE ATT&CK matrix, provides a Diamond Model representation, dissects key incidents (the July 2024 CrowdStrike Phishing Wiper campaign and the March 2026 Stryker Corporation Intune compromise), and conducts an Analysis of Competing Hypotheses (ACH) to justify this state-nexus attribution call. These findings serve to warn corporate defenders that Handala is a highly capable state-nexus adversary, requiring hardened identity management controls, particularly for cloud endpoints and device management servers.

Critical Security Advisory

Handala's evolution to identity-centric "Living-off-the-Cloud" (LotL) attacks represents a critical threat. By targeting Microsoft Intune or Entra ID Global Administrators, the group can trigger mass factory resets on physical hardware without dropping traditional malware. Hardening cloud identity planes with FIDO2 MFA and Privileged Identity Management (PIM) is an urgent priority.
